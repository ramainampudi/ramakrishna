Projects

Dynamic CVV2

A service using which debit and credit card holders can generate a dynamic CVV2, for doing safer online purchases, using various digital banking channels. Lead the initiative on the tech side till implementation. Helps mitigate fraud.

PIN Reminder

A service using which credit card customers can remind themselves their card’s PIN if they have forgotten. Lead the initiative till implementation. Increase in bank's revenue and NPS.

Recurrent Charges

A regulatory requirement where bank is required to give a service to their credit card customers, to block recurrent charges at a merchant level. Lead the project from start to finish including requirement gathering and formulation.

Automatic Warning Bulletin

Warning bulletin is a database of blocked cards used by Mastercard and Visa to decline transactions on behalf of card issuers during stand-in. Designed and developed the entire solution so that the card information is sent to the MC/Visa in realtime as soon as a card is blocked.

Contactless Cards

Part of the contactless cards implementation for a global bank in LATAM, from initiation to implementation, which included various key installations and certifications with MasterCard and Visa.

PIN Migration

Migration of credit card PINs from one processing system of bank to another of a differnt bank. Involves key installation, encryption and transportation of PIN data. Done with zero issues.

Association Compliance

Credit Bureau Reporting
